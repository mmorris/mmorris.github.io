Sync Adium Chat History with Dropbox

Mar 6th, 2012

Ever wished Adium would share its chat history across all your Macs? This script will move your chat history to your Dropbox folder. It will not merge your history from other computers. Just drag that over manually for each account.

Disclaimer: This might not work with future versions of Adium. Use at your own risk.
