Chocolate Espresso Cake

My wife has been perfecting this chocolate espresso cake for the past several years. I love it because it’s not overly sweet and has a really great coffee taste. Here’s how you can make it at home.

Part 1: The Cake

Sift the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup cocoa powder

Then, in a mixing bowl, combine

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Next, slowly add dry to wet while stirring. Mix in the chocolate chips. Pour into a bundt pan and place in over at 350F for 55 minutes.1

When finished, let cool then flip it out of the bundt.

Part 2: The Icing

  • 2 or 3 shots of espresso2
  • 3 oz semisweet baking chocolate
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp light corn syrup3
  • ½ tsp oil

Combine in a saucepan over medium heat. Drizzle over the cake. Take any extra icing and create a reservoir of chocolaty espresso goodness at the center of the cake.

Enjoy!
