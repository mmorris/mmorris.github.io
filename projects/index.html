Projects

Doc Vault

Doc Vault is an iOS app for capturing physical documents on your phone. Snap a picture of a credit card receipt or bank statement, and you can find it later by searching for text found within the document. Get it on the App Store.

FitTweets

FitTweets was a quick python project I threw together to learn about OAuth and the FitBit API. See it live here.

Interception

Interception is a simple app that causes Twitter notifications in OS X Mountain Lion open a Twitter client of your choice. Download the beta here.

Kid Vids

Ever wanted to collect some YouTube videos and give them to your kid but didn’t want to grant them access to all of YouTube? I wrote an iOS app that does that.

Status: I retired Kid Vids from the app store in April of 2013.

Launchctl API

This static library aims to provide some, if not all, of the functionality provided by launchctl. It’s a work-in-progress, and I am not yet caught up to the 10.7.3 version of launchctl. If you just need bsexec, the current version should work for you.

WebTOP

WebTOP was an 3D interactive computer graphics system that simulates and visualizes optical phenomena. It was developed at Mississippi State University between 1997 and 2009. I participated in the project from 1999 to 2005. The 3D models are VRML, and the physics simulations are coded in Java. The code is available here under a non-commercial Creative Commons license.
